Let's design and implement Client Module → HRMS → Interview Management for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Completed Modules:

* Recruitment
* Job Requisitions
* Job Openings
* Candidates
* Offer Management

Interview Management is a submodule of HRMS and must integrate tightly with Recruitment.

Module Hierarchy:

HRMS
└── Interview Management

Module Code:

INTERVIEWS

Purpose:

Manage the complete interview lifecycle from candidate shortlisting to final selection or rejection.

The module must support customizable interview workflows for different positions, departments and companies.

Example:

Software Engineer

Round 1 → Aptitude Test
Round 2 → Technical Interview
Round 3 → Manager Discussion
Round 4 → HR Discussion

Example:

Sales Executive

Round 1 → Telephonic Screening
Round 2 → Group Discussion
Round 3 → HR Discussion

The system must support custom interview pipelines without code changes.
