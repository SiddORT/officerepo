# Payroll Integration

Integrate with Payroll Management.

* Active EMIs
* Outstanding Amount

Gross Salary
− Taxes
− Loan EMI
= Net Salary

After payroll approval:

* Mark installment as paid
* Update outstanding balance

Prevent duplicate deductions.
