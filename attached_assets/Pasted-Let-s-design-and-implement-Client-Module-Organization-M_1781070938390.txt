Let's design and implement Client Module → Organization Management → Companies for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Architecture:

Client
↓
Companies
↓
Departments
↓
Designations
↓
Employees
↓
Assets

Each Client has:

* Dedicated Database
* Dedicated Users
* Dedicated Modules

A Client can own multiple Companies.

Examples:

ABC Group
├── ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd
├── ABC Consulting LLP
└── ABC Logistics Pvt Ltd

The Company entity is the foundation for all future modules including:

* Departments
* Designations
* Employees
* Recruitment
* Onboarding
* Assets
* Payroll
* Compliance
* Billing

This module must be developed as a reusable business module that can be enabled or disabled through Client Module Management.

When enabled:

* Visible in Sidebar
* Routes Accessible
* APIs Accessible

When disabled:

* Hidden from Sidebar
* Routes Blocked
* APIs Blocked

Use existing:

client_modules

module activation framework.
