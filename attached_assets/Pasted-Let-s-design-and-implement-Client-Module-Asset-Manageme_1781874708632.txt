Let's design and implement Client Module → Asset Management → Asset Inventory for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Completed Modules:

* Companies
* Branches
* Departments
* Designations
* Employees

Superadmin manages:

* Asset Categories
* Asset Sub Categories

Clients manage their own Asset Inventory.

Architecture:

Client
↓
Company
↓
Branch
↓
Department
↓
Employee
↓
Asset Inventory

Asset Inventory stores actual client-owned assets.

Examples:

* Laptops
* Desktops
* Monitors
* Printers
* Servers
* Mobile Phones
* SIM Cards
* Access Cards
* Machines
* Vehicles
* Furniture
* Tools
* Software Licenses

Every asset must have a unique QR Code.

Scanning the QR Code should display the complete asset profile.

This module must be developed as a reusable business module controlled through client_modules.

Module Code:

ASSET

When enabled:

* Visible in Sidebar
* Routes Accessible
* APIs Accessible

When disabled:

* Hidden from Sidebar
* Routes Blocked
* APIs Blocked
