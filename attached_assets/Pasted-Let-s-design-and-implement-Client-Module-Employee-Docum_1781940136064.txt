Let's design and implement Client Module → Employee Documents for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Completed Modules:

* Companies
* Branches
* Departments
* Designations
* Employees
* Asset Inventory

Employee Documents will centralize all employee-related files and compliance records.

This module will be used by:

* HR Teams
* Recruitment
* Onboarding
* Payroll
* Compliance
* Audit
* Employee Self Service

Architecture:

Client
↓
Company
↓
Employee
↓
Employee Documents

Each document belongs to an Employee.

This module must be developed as a reusable business module controlled through client_modules.

Module Code:

EMPLOYEE_DOCUMENTS

When enabled:

* Visible in Sidebar
* Routes Accessible
* APIs Accessible

When disabled:

* Hidden from Sidebar
* Routes Blocked
* APIs Blocked

Storage:

Current:

* Local Storage

Future:

* AWS S3

Design storage abstraction accordingly.
