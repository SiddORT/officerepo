* Asset Inventory

* Employees
* Vendors
* Procurement
* Notifications
* Workflow Engine

Module Hierarchy:

Asset Management
└── Asset Maintenance

Module Code:

ASSET_MAINTENANCE

Purpose:

Manage preventive maintenance, corrective maintenance, repairs, warranties, AMC contracts, downtime tracking and complete service history for all assets.

The module must maintain a complete maintenance timeline for every asset and provide visibility into asset health, reliability and maintenance costs.
