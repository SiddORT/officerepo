Let's design and implement Client Module → Asset Management → Asset Returns for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Completed Modules:

* Organization Management
* Employees
* Employee Onboarding
* Exit Management
* Asset Inventory
* Asset Assignments
* Asset Requests

Asset Returns must integrate tightly with:

* Asset Inventory
* Asset Assignments
* Employees
* Exit Management
* Final Settlement
* Notifications
* Workflow Engine

Module Hierarchy:

Asset Management
└── Asset Returns

Module Code:

ASSET_RETURNS

Purpose:

Manage the complete return lifecycle of assigned assets.

The module must support scheduled returns, employee exit returns, replacement returns and damaged/lost asset handling.

Asset returns must update inventory status in real time and maintain a complete audit trail.
