Let's design and implement Client Module → Organization Management for Office Repo.

Context:

Office Repo is a Multi-Tenant SaaS platform.

Architecture:

Client
↓
Companies
↓
Departments
↓
Designations
↓
Employees
↓
Assets

Each Client has:

* Dedicated Database
* Dedicated Users
* Dedicated Modules

A Client can own multiple Companies.

Example:

ABC Group
├── ABC Technologies
├── ABC Consulting
└── ABC Logistics

Every Company can have:

* Departments
* Designations
* Employees
* Assets
* Future HRMS Data

This module will be the foundation for all future HR, Recruitment, Employee, Asset, and Workflow modules.
