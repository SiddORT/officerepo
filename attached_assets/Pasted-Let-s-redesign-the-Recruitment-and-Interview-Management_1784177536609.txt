Let's redesign the Recruitment and Interview Management architecture to create a seamless hiring workflow and eliminate duplicate candidate management.

IMPORTANT

This is an architecture and UI/UX improvement.

Do NOT redesign existing database tables unless absolutely necessary.

Do NOT duplicate candidate information.

Reuse existing APIs wherever possible.

Maintain backward compatibility.

==================================================
NEW HIRING ARCHITECTURE
==================================================

The hiring lifecycle should now follow this flow:

Job Requisition
↓

Job Opening
↓

Candidate Master

↓

Interview Management

↓

Offer Management

↓

Employee Onboarding

↓

Employee Master

Ownership of modules should be clearly separated.

Recruitment owns talent acquisition.

Interview Management owns candidate evaluation and selection.

Employee Onboarding owns employee conversion.

Organization owns Employee Master.

==================================================
RECRUITMENT MODULE
==================================================

Recruitment should ONLY contain:

• Dashboard
• Job Requisitions
• Job Openings
• Candidate Master
• Reports
• Settings

Remove Offer Management from Recruitment.

Recruitment ends once a candidate is ready for interview.

==================================================
CANDIDATE MASTER
==================================================

Candidate Master should become a repository of all candidates.

It should NOT contain interview workflows.

It should NOT contain offer workflows.

It should store only candidate information.

Candidate Master should support multiple applications for the same candidate.

One candidate can apply to multiple job openings.

Do not duplicate candidate records.

RESULT
==================================================

The final architecture should feel like:

Recruitment

↓

Candidate Master

↓

Interview Management (Candidate Workspace)

↓

Offer Management

↓

Employee Onboarding

↓

Employee Master

The user should never feel that they are switching between disconnected modules.

Candidate Workspace should become the single operational screen for HR after recruitment.

The final experience should be comparable to enterprise ATS products such as Greenhouse, Lever, Workday and BambooHR while maintaining the Office Repo branding, design language and existing dark theme.
